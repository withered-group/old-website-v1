Flipped Client

One of the cleanest Minecraft modpack clients with stunning UI and a blazing fast startup time.

Features

No more paywalls

Flipped doesn't sell products - neither does it cost money to get (however, you still must have a premium Minecraft account). No more paid cosmetics (excluding Optifine capes).

Optifine

Customize your character with Optifine's paid capes, boost your frames with their optimizations, zoom in far with a customizable keybind and more.

Essential mods

All your usual mods, with a few special improvements.
To keep our loading times minimal and your FPS high, we don't include large mods like the Replay mod. We recommend Forge for mods like that.

Stunning UI

The most modern, sleek, simple and overall stunning interfaces, menus and UI to keep your eyes from burning when you switch servers.

Session changer

Get logged out? Want to switch accounts? Click the 'session' button on the main menu and enter your account details. Don't worry - none of this is stored and Flipped staff can't access it.
